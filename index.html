GroupStage helps your small group stay connected through announcements, events, and shared resources.

Creating or Joining a Group

Create a new group from the home screen, or join an existing group using an invite code shared by your group leader.

Posting Announcements

Group members can post announcements to share updates with everyone. Tap the + button in your group to create a new announcement.

Managing Events

View all upcoming events in the calendar tab. Create new events with date, time, location, and details to keep everyone informed.

Sharing Resources

Use the Resources tab to share links, documents, and notes with your group. Build a library of helpful materials for your community.
